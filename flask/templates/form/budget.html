Budgeting 101

American Consumer Credit Counseling organization advocates that 35 percent of your gross income should go toward your housing and debt service. Using that figure, if you earn $3,000 per month your spending for housing should be in the range of $900 to $1,050. Not all of that money should go toward your rent, though. Housing costs also include such expenses as your utilities, maintenance and renter's insurance.
